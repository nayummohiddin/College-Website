Courses We Offer

At our college, we offer a wide range of courses in various disciplines.

Intermediate

An intermediate course is designed for learners who have a basic understanding of a subject, but want to take their knowledge to a more advanced level. These courses often cover more complex concepts and may require more in-depth analysis and application of skills.

Degree

A degree level course is a higher education program that is typically completed at a college or university. These courses are designed to provide students with a more advanced and specialized level of knowledge and training in a particular field of study.

Post Graduation

Pursuing a postgraduate degree can help you specialize in a particular field and enhance your expertise. Some popular postgraduate courses include a Master's degree in Business Administration (MBA), Master of Science (MSc), and Doctor of Philosophy (PhD).

Our Facilities

Our facilities include spacious classrooms to ensure a comfortable and productive learning environment.

Library

A library is a collection of materials, books or media that are accessible for use and not just for display purposes. A library provides physical (hard copies) or digital access (soft copies) materials, and may be a physical location or a virtual space, or both.

Electronic Lab

An electronics laboratory equipment list should include a multimeter, oscilloscope, power supplies, function generator, soldering iron, and precision hand tools. The primary measurement tool used for observing a waveform is the oscilloscope. A standalone function generator is essential.

Computer Lab

Computer labs are instrumental in helping students learn, work with software programs, complete assignments and interact with their instructors. Most importantly, these IT labs enable learners to acquire digital skills critical in the modern workplace without having to purchase their own hardware and software.
